About Luxora

❝ At Luxora, we don’t just build homes. We shape worlds that whisper legacy. ❞

Luxora Design House Ltd. was born from a single, defiant belief: luxury should feel like coming home. Founded by Acacia and Shawn Mahon in 2025, our studio redefines what it means to live in elegance. Every design is rooted in vision, shaped with intention, and finished with unforgettable detail. We don’t chase trends—we curate spaces that outlast them.

Our projects begin with storytelling. Whether it’s a mountainside show home or a city-view penthouse, we design for depth, emotion, and impact. Luxora isn’t just a design house—it’s a legacy in the making.
